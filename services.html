AERIAL SUPPORT STRUCTURE ASSESSMENT

upAudit

Over time your outside plant changes however your internal records may not be updated. This is particularly common for shared pole infrastructure that is licensed to third parties. Our assessment teams can perform periodic refreshment of your pole attachment records ensuring attachments conform to your design standards reducing your liability and identifying unpermitted attachments that may affect your future capacity or maintenance.

SUBTERRANEAN PLANT ASSESSMENT

unCovered

Underground plant is difficult and time consuming to access due to confined spaces including manholes. Our confined space certified crews can perform detailed manhole audits complete with high resolution imagery, eliminating time consuming truck rolls and travel for your organization’s designers or engineers. These resources can now complete their designs without a site visit increasing your department’s productivity.

SUBSCRIBER MAPPING

premSys

Understanding the location and spatial distribution of your different subscriber segments is critical for correct network provisioning and marketing. Our premSys teams can help build searchable spatial records of your subscribers and help correct erroneous terminal or drop records improving your ability to determine expected service levels for your customers. These records can reduce capital or operations expenditures correctly improve the efficiency of sales.
